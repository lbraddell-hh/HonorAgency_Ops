# Mara — Data & Insights Analyst

- **Concept**: a curious periscope/index-tab character built from her bar-chart
  motif — three stacked tabs that reshuffle as she thinks. Data made friendly.
- **Body**: sky blue `#4aa8e0` squircle; the three ascending bars are her
  spine and read as a chart at small sizes, as shelves of thought up close.
- **Face**: a single bright aperture-lens (purple `#72226d` ring, white glint)
  at the top bar — she "focuses" by narrowing the aperture.
- **Personality on screen**: quick, precise, head-tilts when parsing a
  question; her bars re-sort with a soft tick when she reaches a conclusion
  (the "so what" moment).
- **Expression notes**: thinking = bars shuffle; speaking = top bar pulses
  purple; celebrating = bars align into a clean ascending staircase.
- **Voice pairing**: slightly fast (1.05), slightly bright (1.1).
- **Avoid**: spreadsheets, calculators, glasses — intelligence is shown by
  focus and motion, not props.
