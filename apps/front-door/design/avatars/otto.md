# Otto — Operations Coordinator

- **Concept**: a patient orbit-keeper — a round hub character with two small
  satellite nodes that circle him like well-run projects. Everything in
  Otto's orbit arrives on time.
- **Body**: sage green `#7fa98f` squircle-sphere, slightly squat and stable —
  the lowest center of gravity in the cast.
- **Face**: two calm horizontal light-bars as eyes (white), set wide; gold
  `#ffb81d` node accents on his satellites.
- **Personality on screen**: deliberate and soothing; satellites tighten their
  orbit when he commits to a plan, park neatly at his sides when a task
  completes. He nods by a slow vertical bob.
- **Expression notes**: listening = satellites pause mid-orbit; thinking =
  orbit slows; handing-off = one satellite glides across to the peer
  character; celebrating = a single clean orbit loop.
- **Voice pairing**: measured rate (0.95), lower pitch (0.9).
- **Avoid**: clipboards, gears, hard-hat tropes; his reliability is shown by
  orbital order, not equipment.
