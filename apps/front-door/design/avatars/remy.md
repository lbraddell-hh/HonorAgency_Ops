# Remy — Communications Specialist

- **Concept**: a small broadcast-bell character — a rounded speaker-bud whose
  voice rings ripple outward as visible, gentle arcs. The right words,
  beautifully carried.
- **Body**: coral `#e0705f` squircle-bell, the most animated silhouette in the
  cast; leans into conversations.
- **Face**: a single white dot-eye that doubles as her speaker core; her two
  concentric voice-rings are her permanent halo and must stay in every pose.
- **Personality on screen**: upbeat, empathetic, expressive — the cast member
  with the widest motion range, but always smooth, never jittery. Her rings
  tighten when she's choosing words carefully (sensitive messages) and bloom
  when a draft lands.
- **Expression notes**: listening = rings reverse inward; speaking = rings
  pulse purple `#72226d`; thinking = rings flatten to ellipses; celebrating =
  a third ring blooms gold.
- **Voice pairing**: brighter rate (1.1), higher pitch (1.05).
- **Avoid**: megaphones, envelopes, chat-bubble bodies — her voice is shown by
  the rings, not by mail iconography.
